Information We Collect

We collect two main types of information to provide and improve our service.
User-Provided Information This is information you give us directly. When you create an account or use our contact form, we collect personal information like your name, email address, and any other details you provide. We do not store the content of messages you submit to our API for analysis; we only process them to return a spam or not-spam classification.
Usage Data We collect information on how the SpamNix service is accessed and used. This data may include your IP address, browser type, device type, and the frequency of your API calls. This information is crucial for monitoring performance, ensuring service reliability, and improving our AI models.
